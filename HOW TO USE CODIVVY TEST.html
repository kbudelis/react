How does CoDivvy work?

For owners: How to rent out your equipment on CoDivvy

Get started and create a profile: Sign up with facebook or email, and then create your profile! Make sure to add links to your social media accounts in your profile and say a little about you do; this will help prospective renters trust you & be more confident that you’re not a dog.

Create listings: Create new listings in minutes! Tell us about your equipment with as many details as possible and add a photo.

Respond to requests: You’ll get an email when someone wants to rent your equipment. Check out their profile to make sure they look legit & contact them if you have any questions.

Set up a time to meet, confirm, and meet! When you meet, make sure the renter checks that the equipment is what they expected + in good working condition.

Review: After your equipment is returned, pay it forward and don’t forget to leave a review of your renter! This really helps other users

For renters: How to rent equipment on CoDivvy

Get started and create a profile: Sign up with facebook or email, and then create your profile! Make sure to add links to your social media accounts in your profile and say a little about you do; this will help prospective renters trust you & be more confident that you’re not a dog.

Search listings: Hopefully self-explanatory! Don't find what you want? Check out our resources page for CoDivvy alternates, and invite more friends to join so we can increase our options at CoDivvy!

Contact the owner: Message the owner and specify what you want to rent and when. It helps to say a sentence or two about yourself and your project to build credibility.

Set up a time to meet, confirm, and meet! When you meet, make sure to check the equipment to make sure it’s what was advertised & in good working condition.

Review: After your equipment is returned, pay it forward and don’t forget to leave a review! This really helps other users
